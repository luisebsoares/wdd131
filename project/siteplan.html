YourPeak Fitness

Your personalized path to fitness — from the gym or from home

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to allow users to schedule training sessions with professional personal trainers. Whether training at home or in the gym, clients can browse trainers, view availability, read reviews, and choose the fitness plan that best fits their goals.

Target Audience Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A busy professional wants to train at home in the evenings. They visit the website to fill out their location and book virtual sessions with a certified trainer.
  • Scenario 2: A new gym-goer is nervous about who to train with. They browse the “Meet Our Staff” page to learn about each trainer’s style and read real client reviews before making a decision.

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: Midnight Blue (#1A1F36) – used for headers, buttons, and the site’s navbar.
  • Accent Color: Vibrant Lime (#A3FF12) – used for hover effects, highlights, and call-to-action buttons.
  • Background: Off-White (#F8F8F8)
  • Text Color: Charcoal (#333333)

Typography

Headings Font: Bebas Neue – applied to all headings (h1–h4) for bold, impactful titles.

Body Font: Poppins – applied to paragraph text, forms, and menus for readability and modern appearance.
